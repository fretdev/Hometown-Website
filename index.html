Visit Lagos

From lively beaches to endless nightlife,
Lagos is the heartbeat of Africa waiting for you to explore.

Top Three activities to do at Lagos

A visit to Lekki Conservation Centre

Experience nature at its finest at Lekki Conservation Centre, where lush greenery meets Africa's longest canopy walkway. Escape the city buzz and discover the wild heart of Lagos in one unforgettable adventure.

A visit to the Badagry Black Heritage Museum

Step into history at the Badagry Black Heritage Museum, where every artifact tells the story of resilience and freedom. Discover the roots of African heritage and the powerful journey of the transatlantic slave trade.

A visit to the National Arts Theatre

Discover the iconic National Arts Theatre, the cultural heartbeat of Lagos where Nigeria's art, music, and dance come alive. Immerse yourself in the nation's creative spirit and witness masterpieces that celebrate African excellence.

Your Guide

Meet your guide at the National Arts Theatre, a passionate cultural expert with years of experience bringing Nigeria's rich heritage to life. His deep knowledge of art, history, and performance ensures an unforgettable journey through the creative soul of Lagos.
